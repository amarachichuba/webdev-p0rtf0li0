About Me

While AI is my main interest, I’m eager to explore every corner of computer science—from algorithms and data structures to software engineering, networking, and cybersecurity. I believe that becoming a well‑rounded technologist is essential for building meaningful and responsible AI systems.

I have hands‑on experience with programming and enjoy experimenting with projects that blend creativity and problem‑solving. Whether it’s building small applications, analyzing data, or learning new frameworks, I’m always excited to expand my skills.

My goal is to contribute to innovative projects that push the boundaries of what technology can do, while continuously growing as a learner in this fast‑evolving field.
